Sneha's Photography

Hello and welcome to my photography webpage! I'm an amateur photographer who likes to go on hikes and take landscape photography, macro shots, and anything in black and white.

Currently I am using a Nikon D7100 body with 18-55mm and 55-300mm lenses.

I also love to travel! Here are some places that I have photographed in:

  • California
  • Northeastern states
  • Hawaii
  • North India
  • South India

The things I look for when taking photos are:

  1. Brightness/Lighting
  2. Color and contrast
  3. Stark features
  4. Hidden details that would otherwise go unnoticed
